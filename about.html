A Multi-Disciplinary Designer, Creative Problem Solver, and Passionate Maker.

Welcome to my online portfolio! I am a dedicated student at the University of Michigan Stamps School of Art and Design , where artistry and innovation intersect. My academic journey in Digital Design, complemented by my minor in User Experience design, has ignited my fervor for a future career in product design, human-centered design, and creative direction. Beyond my creative passions, I am driven by a profound commitment to ethical business practices, sustainability, and accessibility-- values that permeate every aspect of my design philosophy. As you explore my work, I hope you'll witness the embodiment of these principles in my projects, reflecting an aspiration to contribute positively to the world through thoughtful and impactful design.
